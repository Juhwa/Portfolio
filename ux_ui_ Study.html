User Research

Introduction --> Interaction --> Closing

Introduction

During the introduction: brief overview
Explain to users that

  • Their participation is completely voluntary
  • They are free to stop participating at any time
  • If they wish to stop participating this will not negatively affect their relationship with your company/institution

Interaction

Keep the tone relaxed but professional
Provide neutral feedback (you should adapt a neutral stance)
Encourage elaboration, Keep control of the interaction

  • Remaind them about the goals of the interaction and what you plan to do with their date
  • Ask if they have anything else to add
  • Thank them for their participation
